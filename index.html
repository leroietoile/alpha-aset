About us

Welcome to Alpha Aset International School, where we embark on a journey of educational excellence and holistic development. At Alpha Aset, we nurture young minds through a comprehensive curriculum that spans Nursery 1 and 2, Kindergarten 1 and 2, Primary 1 to 6, and Junior High School 1 to 3.

Our commitment to providing a global learning experience is reflected in our multilingual approach, with classes conducted in English, French, and Ewe. This not only enriches linguistic skills but also broadens cultural horizons, preparing our students for a connected world.

Beyond academics, we believe in the power of holistic growth. Our engaging extra activities stimulate creativity, foster teamwork, and instill a love for learning. From arts and sports to science and technology, we offer a diverse range of opportunities for your child to explore their passions.

At Alpha Aset, we understand the importance of a supportive community. Our dedicated faculty ensures a nurturing environment where every child is encouraged to flourish. We invite parents to be active partners in this exciting educational journey, where each day brings new discoveries and achievements.

Choose Alpha Aset International School for a vibrant educational experience that goes beyond textbooks, preparing your child for a future of limitless possibilities. Together, let's build a foundation for success and lifelong learning.
